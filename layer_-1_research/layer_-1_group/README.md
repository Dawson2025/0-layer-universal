# layer_-1_group — Research Layer Organization

This directory contains the organizational structure for the research layer.

## Contents

### `layer_-1_00_layer_registry/`
Layer metadata, configuration, and registry information for layer_-1_research.

### `layer_-1_99_stages/`
Workflow stages for research projects (01-11):
- **01_request_gathering** — Research request specification
- **02_research** — Investigation and discovery
- **03_instructions** — Instruction development
- **04_design** — Design and planning
- **05_planning** — Detailed planning
- **06_development** — Implementation and experimentation
- **07_testing** — Validation and testing
- **08_criticism** — Review and critique
- **09_fixing** — Bug fixes and improvements
- **10_current_product** — Finalized research outputs
- **11_archives** — Completed/archived research

## Organization

Research projects and features are organized at the layer_-1_research root level, while this group manages the layer-wide workflow and metadata.
